WHO AM I?

Zone 6 born and raised, so Omeretta the Great is not excluding me. Growing up so deep in the city allowed me to see all sides of Atlanta, and meet all of its different people. A city in the trees, and a populous so diverse, it is impossible not to fall in love. After graduating from Grady High School (now known as Midtown High) I joined the Georgia Army National Guard to assist in paying for college at the University of North Georgia. I received a Bachelors of Science in Criminal Justice and a Paralegal Certificate from the University of Georgia. Following a short stint in Savannah, I returned to the city I love, and push for a better tomorrow.

WHAT AM I?

Presently a student through the Vets Who Code program. This bootcamp has provided me strong knowledge of GitHub, HTML, and CSS. Pushing forward, I will be diving deep into React, Gatsby and Javascript.

Why am I?

Although my initial plan was to work in the legal field, as many mid-twenties and older people know, things don't always go according to plan. After five years in the legal field I decided it was time to move on. It was not what I expected, and not an environment I personally thrive in. I learned more than I thought was possible, and met many amazing people, but it was not something I could see long term. Tech has always been an interest of mine, and after talking over with friends who are on all different sides from UX to Back End, it was time for me to make the jump to something that truly interested me, and pushed me to learn more and practice.
